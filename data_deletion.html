Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

At Retire Abroad (retireabroad.info), we respect your privacy and your control over personal data. This policy explains how data is handled within our mobile application.

1. No Server Storage

This app does not store any user data on a server. All personal information, preferences, and usage data are stored only on your device.

2. OAuth Sign-In

We use OAuth sign-in (e.g., Google or Apple) solely for session identification and to personalize your experience. We do not retain any OAuth credentials or user details on our servers beyond what is necessary for authentication during use.

3. Deleting the App

Deleting or uninstalling the app from your device removes all locally stored user data, including settings, preferences, and cached information. We cannot recover data after deletion.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at info@retireabroad.info.
