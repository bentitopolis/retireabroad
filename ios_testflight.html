Download ReThaiRe for iOS

Follow these three quick steps on your iPhone or iPad.

This page is best opened on your iPhone or iPad to install via TestFlight.
1
Install TestFlight

Open the App Store on your device and install Apple’s TestFlight app (if you don’t have it yet).

When you're on your iOS device, first install TestFlight from the App Store (if needed), then tap the invitation button below to open TestFlight:

Get TestFlight on the App Store
2
Important
Ignore TestFlight's request to send your email to the developer to invite for internal testing. You do not need to do this (if you do, you will need to wait for our invitation link which will take time for us to send it to you). Once you have TestFlight, you can directly proceed to downloading the app (Step 3). Enjoy!
3
Install the app!

Tap the button below to open TestFlight and install the app. We hope you enjoy and leave feedback!

Open in TestFlight Open Public Install Page

Having trouble? Use the public install page and choose Start Testing.
