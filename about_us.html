About Us

Last updated: August 7, 2025

Retire Abroad is a resource-driven platform focused on helping individuals explore retirement and long-term living options overseas (ReThaiRe has a Thailand focus). Our goal is to provide up-to-date guides, expert insights, and helpful tools so you can make informed decisions about your next adventure. Having raised capital from multiple angel investors, we are eager to proceed to our next stage in app and business development, which includes in person guided components as well as continuing to expand on our services already offered.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the process of retiring abroad by offering comprehensive information, real-life experiences, and practical advice. Our tech stack will eventually bring expert content together with agentic service for trip planning, real estate purchasing and retirement budgeting.

Currently, our A-Z comprehensive service is for the Thailand region, but we seek to serve other regions and cultures in the coming quarters such as Europe, and more of Asia.
